Three Decades of

Founded in 1988 by Mr. Shanmugaraj, MRS Onion & Co. began as a small farming operation in the fertile lands of Perambalur, Tamil Nadu. Over three decades, we have grown into a trusted name for GI Tagged Perambalur shallots — supplying premium quality to households, retailers, and export markets across the globe.
