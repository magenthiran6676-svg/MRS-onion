About Us

Rooted in Legacy. Grown in Tamil Nadu. Delivered to the World.

Who We Are

Third-Generation Family Business.

MRS Onion and Co is a third-generation family business built on decades of trust, trade, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, we are one of South India's most reliable wholesale suppliers of GI-Tagged Perambalur Small Onion and Seed Onion — serving exporters, domestic B2B buyers, and farmers across India and internationally.

From supplying exporters who ship to Singapore, Sri Lanka, and the UAE, to powering domestic B2B supply chains and supporting Tamil Nadu's farming community with quality seed onions each season — MRS Onion and Co stands at the heart of the Perambalur Small Onion trade.

Our Story

Three Generations, One Purpose.

Every great business begins with one courageous decision. Ours began with our founder, Mr. S.M. Ramasamy, who built this business from the ground up with nothing but determination and a deep belief in the quality of Tamil Nadu's agricultural produce. After a brief break, he returned with renewed resolve and restarted the venture — a decision that would go on to shape three generations of our family.

His legacy was carried forward with pride and purpose by his son, Mr. M.R. Shanmugaraajhu, who strengthened the family's roots in Chennai's wholesale markets. Over decades of hard work, he earned the trust of farmers, buyers, and trade networks alike — turning MRS Onion and Co into a name synonymous with reliability, fairness, and quality in the South Indian onion trade.

Today, the third generation stands ready to take that legacy further than ever before. Led by Mr. R.S. Magenthiran, the business is being transformed from a respected regional name into a globally recognised wholesale and export brand — bringing the finest Perambalur Small Onion from the farms of Tamil Nadu to kitchens and markets across the world.

Three generations. Decades of experience. One enduring promise.

What We Do

Our Core Operations.

🌐

Export Supply

We are a trusted supply partner for exporters and international buyers shipping Perambalur Small Onion to Singapore, Sri Lanka, UAE, and other global markets. Our produce is carefully graded, cleaned, and packed to meet international standards, with full documentation support.

🏪

Domestic B2B

We serve domestic buyers including mandis, wholesale distributors, food processors, hotel chains, restaurant groups, cloud kitchens, and institutional buyers across South India and pan-India markets — providing consistent volume throughout the season.

🌱

Seed Onion Trade

Every planting season, we supply premium quality Seed Onions to farmers across Tamil Nadu. Selected for size, health, and germination quality.
*Contact us ahead of planting season for stock & pricing.

🧅

GI-Tagged Speciality

The Perambalur Small Onion holds a GI Tag from the Government of India, guaranteeing authenticity, superior taste, and shelf life. Sourced directly from farms for full traceability.
