Our Story

From a Small Farm
to Global Tables.

In 1988, Mr. Shanmugaraj started with a single vision — to bring the finest small red shallots from the fertile soils of Perambalur to every kitchen that valued authentic flavor. What began as a humble local operation has grown into one of Tamil Nadu's most respected shallot suppliers.

Today, MRS Onion & Co. procures directly from heritage farms across Perambalur, maintaining zero intermediaries to guarantee freshness, authenticity, and fair pricing. Our shallots carry the prestigious GI (Geographical Indication) tag — a testament to their origin and superior quality.

Our Journey

35+ Years of Excellence.

1988

The Beginning

Mr. Shanmugaraj establishes MRS Onion & Co. in Nattarmangalam, Perambalur — sourcing directly from local shallot farmers.

1995

Regional Expansion

Operations scale across Tamil Nadu, supplying to supermarkets, wholesale markets, and catering businesses.

2005

Export Begins

First international shipments to Singapore and Sri Lanka, establishing MRS Onion & Co. as a trusted global supplier.

2015

GI Tag Certification

Perambalur shallots receive Geographical Indication certification — confirming the unmatched regional authenticity of our produce.

2020

North America Markets

Expanded exports to USA and Canada, bringing authentic Perambalur shallots to the Indian diaspora and specialty markets.

Now

Growing Global

Serving four countries with plans to expand further, MRS Onion & Co. remains committed to farm-direct quality and heritage.
